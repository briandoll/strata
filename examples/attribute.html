Enhancing markup you already have

This article is written the way any article is written: headings and paragraphs of plain HTML. Nothing about it depends on JavaScript to be readable. That's the point of progressive enhancement — the baseline works for everyone.

Strata finds the data-strata container after the page loads, tucks the full content away as the deepest level, and wires up the shorter levels you provided. If the script never runs, none of that happens and the reader simply reads the article.

Call Strata.init() to enhance every matching container, or pass a selector and options to target specific ones. Everything else — the dots, the gestures, the keyboard, the announcements — comes along for free.
